What is the Genovaverse?

Since the late 2000s, a scrappy crew of camcorder auteurs out of a Delray Beach, Florida gym has been quietly building one of the internet's most devoted cult followings. It started with Jason Genova — a Star Wars-obsessed, self-declared "Spaniard" chasing a pro bodybuilding card — and grew into an entire ensemble cast of gym regulars, training partners, and hangers-on known to fans as the Delray Misfits. The saga has its own vocabulary ("sickening," "pissening"), its own mythology, and its own devoted fandom who call themselves Genova's Witnesses.

This site pulls together a community-maintained timeline of the era's most legendary uploads. Some tapes below are digitized and ready to play; others are still being tracked down by the archive team. If you spot a missing date or a lost tape, the hunt continues.
